Toolbox for Spatial Analysis on Urban Health

Created by @Dr. Haifeng Niu Contributed by: Prof. Elisabete A. Silva, Ana Seraphim and Adhib Hussain Syed.

We thank Paulo Morgado and Bruno Miranda, the PIs of eMOTIONAL Cities, and the project team for their valuable feedback and support.

This toolbox provides reference cards for spatial analysis of urban health. The reference cards included here are two main types:

  • reference cards of variables, index and metrics related to urban health determinants and outcomes;
  • reference cards of spatial analysis and modelling of urban health.
